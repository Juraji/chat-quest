Scenarios

A scenario in ChatQuest is a defined scene where you’ll interact with AI characters and Non-Player Characters (NPCs). It describes the setting – including the environment, any ongoing events, and who else is present. Think of it as a virtual location with its own story unfolding.

Example: You are sitting at a table in a medieval tavern. You are enjoying a mug of Orcish Ale; While the local bard is playing surprisingly well written tunes.
